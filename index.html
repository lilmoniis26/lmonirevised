About Lil'Monii's Enterprise LLC

Lil'Monii's Enterprise LLC is a founder‑led brand rooted in resilience, creativity, and community impact. Through digital services and on‑the‑ground outreach, we build with purpose and keep people at the center.

Community Cooling Fund

The Community Cooling Fund provides bottled water, electrolyte drinks, snacks, and portable cooling support to people experiencing homelessness during San Antonio's extreme heat.

Every dollar helps us put cold drinks and real relief directly into the hands of our neighbors on the street.

Fans for Families Fund

The Fans for Families Fund provides portable fans to low‑income households without air conditioning—especially seniors, families with children, and vulnerable residents.

Your support helps families stay safer and more comfortable through dangerous summer heat.
