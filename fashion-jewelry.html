Style Tip

The Art of the Stack

Jewelry is the punctuation of every outfit. Here are a few L'Moni rules for wearing yours with confidence and intention:

  • Mix metal tones freely — gold and silver together is modern and intentional, not a mistake.
  • Layer necklaces in varying lengths: choker, mid-length, and long chain creates effortless depth.
  • Stack rings on multiple fingers or multiple on one — the more meaningful, the better.
  • Statement earrings love a simple necklace. Let one piece be the star of the show.
  • Never underestimate the anklet — it's the detail everyone notices last and remembers first.
