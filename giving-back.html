Our Why

Style Is Nothing
Without Substance

L'Moni Maison de Mode was built on a foundation that goes far beyond fashion. We believe that a business which benefits only itself is not truly successful. Real success looks like lifting your community while you rise.

Every collection we launch, every partnership we form, every order that ships from our boutique — a portion of the proceeds flows directly back into the causes and communities that shape who we are as a brand and as people.

We donate to nonprofits serving women, children, and families in San Antonio. We mentor women who dream of starting their own businesses. We hire from the community. We show up — not for the credit, but because it is simply the right thing to do.

Nonprofits We Stand Behind

These are the organizations we actively fund, volunteer with, and champion. Your purchases help keep their doors open and their missions alive.

Women's Empowerment
♀️
San Antonio Women's Shelter & Resource Center
Providing safe housing, counseling, job training, and dignity to women and children fleeing domestic violence in Bexar County. L'Moni donates clothing, beauty essentials, and monetary support quarterly.
L'Moni Impact: $12,000+ donated · 200+ clothing items
Youth Education
📚
SA Girls Who Lead
An afterschool leadership and mentorship program for girls ages 10–18 in underserved San Antonio communities. Teaching confidence, career readiness, and self-worth through workshops, field trips, and mentorship.
L'Moni Impact: $8,500+ donated · 3 workshops sponsored
Food Security
🥗
San Antonio Food Bank — Women & Families
Supporting the women-and-family-focused initiatives of the San Antonio Food Bank, ensuring that no family in our city goes hungry. A percentage of every holiday collection sale goes directly here.
L'Moni Impact: $9,000+ donated · Holiday Drive Sponsor
Second Chances
🌱
Project REBOOT — Workforce Reentry
Helping women re-entering the workforce after incarceration with professional clothing, resume support, interview coaching, and connections to employers willing to give second chances. L'Moni is a founding clothing sponsor.
L'Moni Impact: 150+ women dressed for interviews
Mental Health
💛
Thrive SA — Women's Mental Wellness
Providing free and low-cost mental health services, therapy groups, and wellness workshops for women in San Antonio who cannot afford traditional therapy. L'Moni sponsors their quarterly wellness events.
L'Moni Impact: $6,000+ · 4 events sponsored annually
Housing & Stability
🏠
Haven House SA — Transitional Housing
Supporting women and children transitioning out of homelessness with 6–12 month stable housing, life skills training, and community. L'Moni furnishes rooms with personal care essentials and clothing.
L'Moni Impact: $7,500+ donated · 80+ care packages
The L'Moni Entrepreneur Program

We Built From Nothing.
Now We Help You Build.

The L'Moni Entrepreneur Program exists because we know what it feels like to have a vision and no roadmap. We've been there — and we found our way through. Now we open that door for other women who are just getting started.

The program is completely free and open to women in the San Antonio area who are in the early stages of building a business — whether it's a boutique, a service, a product, or anything in between.

  • One-on-one mentorship sessions with the L'Moni founder — 60 minutes, no cost
  • Business plan review, feedback, and strategic guidance
  • Connections to local resources, investors, and business networks
  • Access to micro-grant opportunities for qualifying applicants
  • Invitation to the annual L'Moni Entrepreneur Showcase event
  • A community of women who are building alongside you
